NOR4 Tokenomics

1. Overview

NOR4 is the official digital token of the NEST4 investment ecosystem.

The tokenomics of NOR4 are designed with a long-term vision, focusing on sustainability, transparency, and alignment with the NEST4 digital investment fund.

Rather than emphasizing speculative mechanics, the structure prioritizes stability, clarity, and ecosystem integrity.

⸻

2. Token Purpose

NOR4 serves as a core component of the NEST4 ecosystem by:
	•	Representing participation within the NEST4 framework
	•	Aligning holders with the long-term investment vision
	•	Supporting ecosystem sustainability
	•	Acting as a bridge between the community and the NEST4 fund

The token is designed to exist independently in the market while maintaining strategic relevance to the fund.
